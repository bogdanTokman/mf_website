Our
features

MailFire is a powerful, easy-to-use marketing tool designed to fulfill your potential

Create and Manage

An email creator empowered with our drag-and-drop principle allows you to create and send emails easily

Dynamic Content

Send custom campaigns or use advanced features such as showing several products or segments in one email

RSS

Integrate all marketing platforms (blog, website or social media) to deliver up-to-date content to your customers daily

Preview

Test campaigns and check newsletters on your mobile device before sending it to the entire mailing list

Deliverability

MailFire offers a high delivery rate as all the campaigns are designed according to legal regulations and ethical practices

Outgoing Speed

Send millions of messages with our high quality-hosted solution and successfully deliver them to the customers

Email Analysis

Get access to the detailed analysis that allow you to check broken links and mages or revise the HTML code

Authentication

Authenticate your campaigns with domain identification records, and minimize the effect of antispam software

Dedicated IP Warm Up

Gain ISP confidence and increase your delivery rate with IP addresses with the reputation adjusted to your goals

Made by People

Work with people who conduct daily analyzes to maintain the process effectiveness and ensure the antispam standards

A/B and split Testing

Mailfire allows you to create unlimited split tests and get the best response from your customers

Optimize your Campaigns

Create 2 versions of mailing campaigns, set up MailFire to test them and send the winning one an hour later

Control Process

Customize your A/B testing criteria by choosing sample percentage, test duration, and winner metrics

Improve Performance

Expand your A/B testing and split-test your contents, newsletter designs, senders to get a higher response rate

Get Real-Time Result

Track your A/B test by getting access to the split-test report and see which newsletter is edging out
